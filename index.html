Made for people who hate app-hopping.

Travel today is still fragmented.

Airport cab in one app, flight in another, hotel in a third, and payments… somewhere else. You enter the same info again and again—and when plans change, you’re left fixing a domino mess.

  • • Switch between 3–5 apps for a single trip
  • • Manual rebooking after delays/cancellations
  • • Missed offers because the “right card” isn’t suggested

Synk fixes this in one flow

  1. Understand: Tell Synk your plan in plain English. It reads your calendar too (only if you allow).
  2. Plan: Synk finds the best options and schedules—end to end.
  3. Execute: It books, reschedules, and pays automatically with your saved preferences.
